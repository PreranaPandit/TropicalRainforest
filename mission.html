OUR MISSION/VISION

Protecting nature, for people today and future generations.

The mission of our trust is to conserve the plants and animals that are important to both plants and animals. As it took 60 and 100 million years to evolve and are believed to be oldest and complex ecosystem on earth.

WHY ARE RAINFORETS IMPORTANT?
THEY PROVIDE A HABITAT FOR PLANTS AND ANIMALS



  1. Biodiversity

Tropical forests cover only twelve percent of the land-area of the Earth, yet they are home of species but because of tropical deforestation, at least one species gets dissapeared every day which must be stopped.

  1. Medicines

Rainforest is a vital source of medicine that have been tested for pharmaceutical properties. Most were first discovered and and used by indigenous people.

  1. Food Diversity

It offers a bounty of foods. Without rainforests, this opportunity is lost, as is the chance to develop entirely new food plants.
